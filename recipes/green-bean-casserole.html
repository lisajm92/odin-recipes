Green Bean Casserole

Description

This is my favorite holiday side and it's easy as hell. I replace milk with broth so I don't die.

Ingredients

  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 3/4 cup chicken or beef broth
  • 2 cans green beans
  • 1 1/3 cups french fried onions

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 350. Mix it all together except some of the onions. Put it in a baking dish and bake for 30 min.
  • Take out, top with remaining onions, and bake for 5 more minutes.
